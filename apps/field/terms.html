These Terms & Conditions govern the text-message (SMS/MMS) program operated by Roybal Construction, LLC (“Roybal Construction,” “we,” “us,” or “our”). By providing your mobile number and opting in, you agree to these terms.

Program description

When you opt in, we send transactional, job-related text messages about the restoration or construction services you have engaged us for — for example: technician “on our way” arrival notices, appointment and walkthrough reminders, drying-equipment pickup notices, and job-status updates. This is not a marketing or promotional program.

How you opt in

You opt in by providing your mobile number and giving consent in our signed Work Authorization & Service Agreement, or verbally when you request service. Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies and depends on the status and activity of your job.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply. Your mobile carrier’s standard message and data rates apply to each message you send or receive. Roybal Construction does not charge for the text messages themselves.

Opt out & help
